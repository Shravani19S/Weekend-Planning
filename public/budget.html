Fun on a Budget

Fun doesn’t have to be expensive! Here are some of the best free or low-cost activities in your area to enjoy with family and friends.

Free Outdoor Yoga

Join a free outdoor yoga class at the park, suitable for all skill levels. Bring your mat and enjoy the fresh air.

Farmers' Market

Support local farmers and artisans at the weekend farmers' market. Entry is free, and you’ll find delicious, fresh produce.

Community Art Walk

Explore local art galleries and street murals at the community art walk. The perfect activity for art lovers.

Park Picnic

Enjoy a peaceful day at the park with a picnic. Relax with your family or friends, surrounded by nature.
